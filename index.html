HOW LITMUS WORKS

Four steps, and the code comes last.

MATCH

Evidence finds the issue

Your public work is matched to open, unassigned issues — biased toward under-served repos and maintainers who registered here asking for help.

ORIENT

A tour before you touch anything

Litmus reads the implicated files, their callers, the tests, and the constraints that trip people up — and briefs you in sixty seconds.

PLAN

Your approach meets the real code

State your intended approach. Litmus surfaces the specific collisions a naive plan would miss, tied to real functions with file:line evidence.

BRIEF

Your PR arrives pre-explained

The maintainer gets a trust brief: what the change does, whether you revised when corrected, and one sortable verdict.

Connected once. Remembered after.

On first connection Litmus builds a durable map of the repo — modules, dependency edges, tests, conventions, constraint signals — and refreshes it only when the default branch moves.
